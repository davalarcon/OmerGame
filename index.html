INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Want to learn the numbers in hebrew?
  2. Click "Start Game" to begin.
  3. From the main grid at the center, click on the correct hebrew number that matches the number and character that appears on the left side.
  4. When you reach to 50, without making mistakes under 60 seconds, you win!
  5. Ready for level 2? Click on the 'Turn Off Cards', and the card on the left will disappear.
  6. Need extra time to practice? Click on the "Stop Timer" to frezee the count down.
